**Startup Ritual (5 min):**

4. Open the file. Don't check email.

**Shutdown Ritual (5 min):**
1. Stop. Resist "just one more thing."
2. Save your work.
3. Park downhill — write tomorrow's action.
4. Close all project tabs.
5. Acknowledge you showed up.
