## How to fire it

Two ways:

**AI-led (recommended):** Run `/unstuck pricing-iteration`. The AI walks you through the Socratic flow, captures your answers, and fills the matching section in your User Context file.

**Paper-led:** Print `T25_The_Pricing_Iteration.pdf` from your Ship It Kit downloads, fill it in by hand, and save it where future-you will find it.

Most builders do both — AI for the thinking, PDF for the paper trail.
