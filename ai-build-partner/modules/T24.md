# T24 — The Support / Refund Playbook

**Phase:** Module 7 — Day 35
**Underlying module:** `support-refund` (lives in **Marketing OS** expansion, not Kit)
**Printable PDF:** `T24_The_Support_Refund_Playbook.pdf` (in your Ship It Kit downloads)

## What this template does

Refund policy + 12 canned response templates before you hit your first refund request.

## How to fire it

Three paths, depending on what the buyer owns:

**1. Buyer has Bundle (Kit + Marketing OS):** Run `/unstuck support-refund` directly. The full Socratic module is loaded.

**2. Buyer has Kit only (no Marketing OS):** Tell them: "T24 fires the `support-refund` module — that's in Marketing OS, which you don't have installed. Two options: (a) print `T24_The_Support_Refund_Playbook.pdf` from your Kit downloads and fill it in by hand, or (b) grab Marketing OS at shipitwithmolly.gumroad.com/l/marketing-os to unlock the full AI version of this + 9 more marketing skills." Then offer a lighter conversational fallback based on the template's general framing.

**3. Buyer wants conversational help anyway (no MOS):** Run a brief Socratic flow using the template's PDF questions. It won't have MOS's depth (no Hormozi/Belcher/Schwartz frameworks loaded), but it gets them moving.

## Skill behavior

When invoked as `/unstuck T24`, check if `modules/support-refund.md` exists in the user's installed skill folder.
