## What this template does

Smoke-test pass/pivot/kill criteria for demand-risk-primary buyers. Validate willingness-to-pay before you build.

## How to fire it

Two ways:

**AI-led (recommended):** Run `/unstuck smoke-test`. The AI walks you through the Socratic flow, captures your answers, and fills the matching section in your User Context file.

**Paper-led:** Print `T16_The_Smoke_Test_Decision.pdf` from your Ship It Kit downloads, fill it in by hand, and save it where future-you will find it.

Most builders do both — AI for the thinking, PDF for the paper trail.
