# T10 — The Stuck Toolkit

**Phase:** Daily, Modules 3–4 (whenever stuck)
**Underlying module:** `stuck`
**Printable PDF:** `T10_The_Stuck_Toolkit.pdf` (in your Ship It Kit downloads)

## What this template does

Diagnose which of the 4 Stuck Patterns is in your way (Scope Spiral, Validation Loop, Build Avoidance, Launch Paralysis), then prescribe the right unstick.

## How to fire it

Two ways:

**AI-led (recommended):** Run `/unstuck stuck`. The AI walks you through the Socratic flow, captures your answers, and fills the matching section in your User Context file.

**Paper-led:** Print `T10_The_Stuck_Toolkit.pdf` from your Ship It Kit downloads, fill it in by hand, and save it where future-you will find it.

Most builders do both — AI for the thinking, PDF for the paper trail.

## Skill behavior

When invoked as `/unstuck T10`, this alias routes the buyer to `stuck`. Tell the buyer: "T10 fires the stuck module — running that now." Then load `modules/stuck.md` and execute it.
