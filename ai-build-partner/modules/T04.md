# T04 — The Validation Scorecard

**Phase:** Module 1 — Day 2
**Underlying module:** `validate`
**Printable PDF:** `T04_The_Validation_Scorecard.pdf` (in your Ship It Kit downloads)

## What this template does

RICE-style scoring of your idea before you build it. Customer conversations + kill/pivot/go decision at the end.

## How to fire it

Two ways:

**AI-led (recommended):** Run `/unstuck validate`. The AI walks you through the Socratic flow, captures your answers, and fills the matching section in your User Context file.

**Paper-led:** Print `T04_The_Validation_Scorecard.pdf` from your Ship It Kit downloads, fill it in by hand, and save it where future-you will find it.

Most builders do both — AI for the thinking, PDF for the paper trail.

## Skill behavior

When invoked as `/unstuck T04`, this alias routes the buyer to `validate`. Tell the buyer: "T04 fires the validate module — running that now." Then load `modules/validate.md` and execute it.
