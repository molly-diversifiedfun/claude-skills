# T13 — The Tech Stack

**Phase:** Module 4 — Day 21
**Underlying module:** `pick-my-stack`
**Printable PDF:** `T13_The_Tech_Stack.pdf` (in your Ship It Kit downloads)

## What this template does

Pick the simplest stack that ships in 10 days. Boring tech wins. No yak-shaving.

## How to fire it

Two ways:

**AI-led (recommended):** Run `/unstuck pick-my-stack`. The AI walks you through the Socratic flow, captures your answers, and fills the matching section in your User Context file.

**Paper-led:** Print `T13_The_Tech_Stack.pdf` from your Ship It Kit downloads, fill it in by hand, and save it where future-you will find it.

Most builders do both — AI for the thinking, PDF for the paper trail.

## Skill behavior

When invoked as `/unstuck T13`, this alias routes the buyer to `pick-my-stack`. Tell the buyer: "T13 fires the pick-my-stack module — running that now." Then load `modules/pick-my-stack.md` and execute it.
