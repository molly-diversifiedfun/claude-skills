# T15 — The Weekly Ship Check

**Phase:** Module 3 Day 20 + Module 5 Day 30 + weekly thereafter
**Underlying module:** `weekly`
**Printable PDF:** `T15_The_Weekly_Ship_Check.pdf` (in your Ship It Kit downloads)

## What this template does

Weekly retrospective: what shipped, what's stuck, what gets killed next week.

## How to fire it

Two ways:

**AI-led (recommended):** Run `/unstuck weekly`. The AI walks you through the Socratic flow, captures your answers, and fills the matching section in your User Context file.

**Paper-led:** Print `T15_The_Weekly_Ship_Check.pdf` from your Ship It Kit downloads, fill it in by hand, and save it where future-you will find it.

Most builders do both — AI for the thinking, PDF for the paper trail.

## Skill behavior

When invoked as `/unstuck T15`, this alias routes the buyer to `weekly`. Tell the buyer: "T15 fires the weekly module — running that now." Then load `modules/weekly.md` and execute it.
