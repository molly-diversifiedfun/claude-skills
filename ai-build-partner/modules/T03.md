# T03 — The Project Selector

**Phase:** Day 0 — Module 0 (also Day 30 next-project decision)
**Underlying module:** `discovery`
**Printable PDF:** `T03_The_Project_Selector.pdf` (in your Ship It Kit downloads)

## What this template does

Score 3–5 candidate projects on Energy / Shippability / Proof (1–5 each). Highest total wins. Pick the one you'll actually finish.

## How to fire it

Two ways:

**AI-led (recommended):** Run `/unstuck discovery`. The AI walks you through the Socratic flow, captures your answers, and fills the matching section in your User Context file.

**Paper-led:** Print `T03_The_Project_Selector.pdf` from your Ship It Kit downloads, fill it in by hand, and save it where future-you will find it.

Most builders do both — AI for the thinking, PDF for the paper trail.

## Skill behavior

When invoked as `/unstuck T03`, this alias routes the buyer to `discovery`. Tell the buyer: "T03 fires the discovery module — running that now." Then load `modules/discovery.md` and execute it.
