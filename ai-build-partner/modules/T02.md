# T02 — The Time Protection Plan

**Phase:** Before Day 1
**Underlying module:** `time-protect`
**Printable PDF:** `T02_The_Time_Protection_Plan.pdf` (in your Ship It Kit downloads)

## What this template does

Block real recurring hours on your calendar. DND on. Minimum 7 hrs/week with the AI Build Partner, 10 hrs/week without.

## How to fire it

Two ways:

**AI-led (recommended):** Run `/unstuck time-protect`. The AI walks you through the Socratic flow, captures your answers, and fills the matching section in your User Context file.

**Paper-led:** Print `T02_The_Time_Protection_Plan.pdf` from your Ship It Kit downloads, fill it in by hand, and save it where future-you will find it.

Most builders do both — AI for the thinking, PDF for the paper trail.

## Skill behavior

When invoked as `/unstuck T02`, this alias routes the buyer to `time-protect`. Tell the buyer: "T02 fires the time-protect module — running that now." Then load `modules/time-protect.md` and execute it.
