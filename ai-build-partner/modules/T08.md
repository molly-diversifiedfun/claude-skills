**Phase:** Module 2 — Day 10 + Module 3 — Day 11

## What this template does

Day-by-day build schedule for Days 11–20. What ships, what's a stub, what's deferred to V2.

## How to fire it

Two ways:

**AI-led (recommended):** Run `/unstuck sprint`. The AI walks you through the Socratic flow, captures your answers, and fills the matching section in your User Context file.

**Paper-led:** Print `T08_The_10_Day_Sprint_Plan.pdf` from your Ship It Kit downloads, fill it in by hand, and save it where future-you will find it.

Most builders do both — AI for the thinking, PDF for the paper trail.

## Skill behavior

When invoked as `/unstuck T08`, this alias routes the buyer to `sprint`. Tell the buyer: "T08 fires the sprint module — running that now." Then load `modules/sprint.md` and execute it.
