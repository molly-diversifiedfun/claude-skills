# Build Irresistible Offer

**Take any paid offer — new or existing — and rebuild it into something people feel stupid saying no to.**

## The Problem

Most offers don't fail because they're too expensive. They fail because the buyer can't picture the outcome, doesn't believe it's achievable for them, and isn't sure what they actually get for the money. The fix isn't a discount or a bigger guarantee — it's an offer architecture that maximizes dream outcome, perceived speed, and certainty while collapsing risk.

## What It Does

Runs the Hormozi value-equation methodology as a guided session. Outputs a complete offer blueprint:

- **Value stack** — every component named, priced, and ordered
- **Named IP** — your method has a name, not just a description
- **Guarantee structure** — risk reversal calibrated to objection
- **Urgency mechanics** — real, ethical scarcity built into the offer
- **Selling statements** — the lines you actually say in sales conversations or copy
- **Bonus architecture** — what bonuses to add, in what order, to break the perceived value ceiling

Works for SaaS, courses, services, coaching, productized consulting, info products, communities, physical products. Does NOT work as a substitute for product-market fit — if the underlying offer is wrong, this will package it well but it still won't sell.

## What Makes This Different

- **Specific to the Hormozi value equation**, not generic copywriting
- **Refuses vague inputs** — if you can't name your dream outcome, the skill makes you name it before continuing
- **Names your IP** — most offer skills skip this; named methodology is the single biggest move for premium pricing
