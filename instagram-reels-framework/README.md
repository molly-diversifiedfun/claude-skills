# Instagram Reels Framework

**Stop guessing what hook to open with, what beats to hit, and whether the script is actually good before you film. Get a Reel script timed to the second, with a 12-point quality score before you pick up the phone.**

## The Problem

Most Reels die in the first 1.5 seconds because the hook didn't earn the swipe-stop. The other 90% die because the beat structure is flat — there's no escalation, no payoff, no reason to share. AI-generated Reel scripts almost always fail these tests because they default to listicle structure and explanatory voice. This skill bakes the structure in BEFORE generation.

## What It Does

Three-phase guided session:

**Phase 1 — Brand + niche research.** You name your brand, niche, and goal (DM conversions, link clicks, follow growth, saves). The skill calibrates everything downstream from these three inputs.

**Phase 2 — Strategy selection.** Picks from:
- **7 hook types** — contrarian, pattern interrupt, callout, problem-agitate, curiosity gap, transformation, micro-controversy
- **5 beat structures** — hook → tension → resolution variations
- **Multiple emotional arcs** — matched to your goal (sharable arcs differ from saveable arcs)

**Phase 3 — Script generation.** Outputs:
- **Timed script** — beat by beat, second by second
- **Filming notes** — what to shoot, what energy, where to look
- **Screenshot line** — one specific line engineered to be the share-trigger frame
- **Caption** — written for the algorithm, not for the audience
- **12-point scoring rubric** — runs automatically; flags weaknesses with specific fix recommendations

## What Makes This Different

- **Goal-aware scoring.** A "shareable" Reel scores differently than a "DM-conversion" Reel. The rubric calibrates.
- **Screenshot line is engineered, not improvised.** That's the frame people actually share to Stories.
- **Filming notes are specific.** Not "make it engaging" — "look at camera, mid-energy, no smile until beat 4."
- **Has an Unstuck preset** for shippers/builders niche, but works for any brand.
