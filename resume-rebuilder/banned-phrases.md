# Banned Phrases and Words

These are hardcoded constraints. Never use any of these in generated resume or cover letter content.

## Banned Action Verbs

- spearheaded
- leverage / leveraged
- synergize / synergized
- facilitate / facilitated
- optimize / optimized (use "improved" or describe the specific improvement)
- streamline / streamlined
- pioneer / pioneered
- champion / championed
- orchestrate / orchestrated
- innovate / innovated
- transform / transformed
- catalyze / catalyzed
- enhance / enhanced
- strategize / strategized

## Banned Summary Phrases

- "Results-driven professional"
- "Proven track record"
- "Dynamic leader"
- "Strategic thinker"
- "Passionate about"
- "Rockstar"
- "Self-starter"
- "Team player"
- "Detail-oriented" (as standalone descriptor)
- "Go-getter"
- "Thought leader"

## Banned Summary Openings

Never open a professional summary with:
- "Accomplished [role] with [N] years of experience in [gerund]"
- "Results-driven [role] with a proven track record of..."
- "Dynamic and motivated [role] seeking..."
- "Highly experienced [role] dedicated to..."

## Banned Filler

- "responsible for" (describe what you actually did)
- "assisted with" (describe your specific contribution)
- "helped to" (describe the action directly)
- "various" / "multiple" (use the actual number)
- "etc." (list the actual items)
