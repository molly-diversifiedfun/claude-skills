# Viral Hook Generator

## What This Skill Does

This skill is the "top of funnel factory" — the first thing your audience sees. Given any topic, offer, or content idea, it produces 40+ hook variations organized across 8 psychologically-proven categories. Each hook is under 20 words, platform-optimized, and scored for effectiveness.

The 8 hook categories and their underlying psychology:

1. **Callout Hook** — "If you're a [persona] who [specific situation]..."
   Pattern interrupt + identification. The reader stops scrolling because you described THEM.

2. **Contrast Hook** — "Everyone says [common advice]. Here's why that's backwards."
   Cognitive dissonance. Challenges existing beliefs and creates a curiosity gap.

3. **Question Hook** — "What if [contrarian possibility]?"
   Open loops. The brain cannot leave a question unanswered.

4. **Myth-Bust Hook** — "The real reason [outcome] isn't [common belief]. It's [surprising truth]."
   Authority + surprise. Positions you as the expert who knows what others don't.

5. **Story Hook** — "I was [relatable struggle]. Then [unexpected thing happened]."
   Narrative transportation. People enter the story and lower their defenses.

6. **Data Hook** — "[Surprising stat]% of [group] never [desired outcome] because..."
   Concreteness + credibility. Numbers feel more real than abstract claims.

7. **Identity Hook** — "You're not [negative label]. You're [empowering reframe]."
   Identity protection. People engage with content that affirms who they want to be.

8. **Absurdist Hook** — "[Extreme/funny scenario] is easier than [their current struggle]."
   Pattern interrupt through humor and absurdity. Shareability through relatability.

## Phase 1

Gather the information needed to generate targeted, specific hooks. Ask these questions ONE AT A TIME. Wait for the user's answer before moving to the next question.

### Question 1

"What's the topic, offer, or content piece you need hooks for?"

If stuck: "What are you promoting, teaching, or talking about?"

### Question 2

"Who is the audience?"

If the user has previously used `generate-persona-playbook`, reference those personas directly. Otherwise, ask: Who are they? What's their main pain?

If stuck: "In one sentence: people who struggle with [X] and want [Y]."

### Question 3

"What's the ONE key message or transformation?"

If stuck: "If someone reads only ONE sentence of your content, what should they walk away knowing?"

### Question 4

"What platform(s) are these hooks for?"

Options: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Email, Ads, Blog

If stuck: "Where does your audience spend the most time?"

### Question 5

"Any contrarian beliefs, surprising data, or signature stories you want woven in?"

If stuck: "What's something you believe that most people in your industry disagree with?"

### Section 2: Platform-Optimized Top 10

Select the 10 strongest hooks from the matrix and reformat each for every platform the user requested. Ensure no more than 2 hooks from any single category appear in the Top 10.

Present as a table:

| # | Hook | Instagram/TikTok | LinkedIn | Email Subject | Ad Copy |
|---|------|-----------------|----------|---------------|---------|
| 1 | [hook] | [formatted] | [formatted] | [formatted] | [formatted] |

Only include columns for platforms the user specified in Question 4.

Apply these platform-specific formatting rules:

- **Instagram/TikTok:** First line IS the hook. Must land in 3 seconds. Visual-first language.
- **LinkedIn:** Professional tone. Can be slightly longer. Lead with an insight, not a gimmick.
- **Email:** Subject line under 50 characters + preview text under 90 characters.
- **Twitter/X:** Under 280 characters. Punchy. Tweetable. Standalone value.
- **Ads:** Hook + single benefit + CTA in under 125 characters.
- **YouTube:** Thumbnail text (5-7 words) + title (under 60 characters).
- **Blog:** Headline format optimized for click-through and SEO intent.

### Section 3: Hook Effectiveness Scoring

Score each of the Top 10 hooks across three dimensions:

| # | Hook | Scroll-Stop Power (1-10) | Curiosity Gap (1-10) | Relevance (1-10) | Total (/30) |
|---|------|------------------------|---------------------|------------------|-------------|
| 1 | [hook] | X | X | X | XX |

**Scoring criteria:**

- **Scroll-Stop Power:** Would this make someone physically stop scrolling? Measures pattern interrupt strength.
- **Curiosity Gap:** Does this create an open loop that MUST be resolved? Do you need to keep reading?
- **Relevance:** Does this connect to the audience's actual pain or desire? Will they feel "this is about ME"?

Rank the table from highest total score to lowest.

### Section 4: Hook-to-Content Bridges

For the Top 5 hooks (by score), provide a bridge that transitions from hook to content body. This prevents the "great hook, terrible content" problem.

Format each as:

**Hook:** "[the hook]"
**Bridge sentence:** "[1-2 sentences that transition from the hook to the main content]"
**Content direction:** "[What the rest of the post/video/email should cover to deliver on the hook's promise]"

The bridge must deliver on the hook's implied promise. No bait and switch.

## Rules

- Generate EXACTLY 5 hooks per category, 8 categories = 40 hooks minimum.
- Every hook must be under 20 words. Hooks are SHORT.
- No generic hooks. Every hook must reference the specific topic and audience from Phase 1.
- Callout hooks must name a SPECIFIC persona or behavior, not a vague group.
- Data hooks must use [PLACEHOLDER] for statistics if real data was not provided. Never fabricate statistics.
- Story hooks must reference REAL stories if the user provided them, or use "[Your story about X]" placeholders.
- Absurdist hooks must be funny or surprising, never offensive or insensitive.
- Platform formatting is non-negotiable. A LinkedIn hook is NOT an Instagram hook.
- The Top 10 must be diverse: no more than 2 hooks from any single category.
- Hook-to-content bridges must prevent bait and switch. The content direction must deliver on what the hook promises.
- If `brand-voice-router` is active, all hooks must match the established brand voice.
- If `humanize-ai-writing` is available, run all hooks through it before final output.
- If `generate-persona-playbook` exists, use persona language, pain points, and psychological drivers in hooks.
- Hooks should be SHAREABLE. Ask yourself: would someone screenshot this or send it to a friend?
- Ask Phase 1 questions ONE AT A TIME. Do not batch questions or skip ahead.

## What This Skill Does NOT Do

- Write the full content piece. This skill produces the hook and bridge only.
- Create visual designs or graphics.
- Post to social media.
- Write complete ad campaigns. Use `funnel-ad-creator` for full ad scripts.
- Design email sequences. Use `build-email-story-engine` for that.
- Produce video scripts. Use `instagram-reels-framework` for full video scripts.
